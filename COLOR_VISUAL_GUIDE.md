## 💡 Key Differences

| Element | Tenant | Central | Difference |
|---------|--------|---------|-----------|
| **Sidebar** | Dark | Darker | Central is more formal |
| **Header** | Bright white | Dark | Tenant is friendly |
| **Badge Icon** | 🏘️ House | 🏛️ Building | Different identity |
| **Badge Color** | Green | Cyan | Different palette |
| **Background** | Warm off-white | Cool blue-tint | Different tone |
| **Primary Button** | Purple | Deep Blue | Different authority |
| **Accent** | Sky Blue | Cyan | Different style |
| **Feeling** | Community | Government | Different purpose |

---

## 🎨 Color Psychology

### Tenant App (Purple & Green)
- **Purple** = Trust, creativity, community
- **Green** = Growth, positivity, health
- **Light BG** = Approachable, friendly
- **Overall**: "We're here for your community"

### Central App (Blue & Cyan)
- **Deep Blue** = Government, authority, trust
- **Cyan** = Modern, tech, strategic
- **Blue-tint BG** = Official, professional
- **Overall**: "We oversee your city"
